✧ Our Story ✧

Just a girl,
a kitchen, and a lot of butter

SugarWisp started as a weekend obsession — testing recipes, tweaking ratios, and sharing everything with anyone who'd try it. What began as "just baking for fun" turned into something that felt a little magical.

Every item is made fresh in small batches, with real ingredients and a lot of care. No shortcuts. No assembly lines. Just recipes that took many failed attempts to get exactly right ^ - ^

Whether it's a box of cookies for yourself or a treat to gift someone special, we hope every bite brings a little warmth.
